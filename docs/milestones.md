# DraftHQ Milestones

## Milestone 1 - Local MVP
Status: Complete

## Milestone 2 - Multiplayer Draft Room
Goal: Multiple owners can join and draft from phones/laptops.

## Milestone 3 - Live Draft Reliability
Goal: Refresh, reconnect, timer, undo, and wrong-owner protection are stable.

## Milestone 4 - FanDraft-Comparable Experience
Goal: League branding, team logos, TV mode, strong on-the-clock presentation.

## Milestone 5 - League Identity Platform
Goal: League home, records, history, reports, owner/team profiles.
